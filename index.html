About Me

Get to know me better

Hello! I'm Rendi

a Computer Science student who is very curious about how technology, computing, and research can create real impact-especially in areas related to sustainability and social development. I am currently involved in organizations such as IEEE and SSRS, where I get to collaborate on technology and research-based projects that push me to learn and grow every day.

My current focus is on building solid skills in programming, data analysis, and collaborative research. I enjoy learning through experience-whether it's coding for a small project, helping organize a research activity, or just diving into a new topic that interests me.

But outside the world of coding and research, I'm also someone who enjoys the little things. I find a lot of comfort in watching movies-especially movies that make you think deeper. Music is my escape, whether it's soft music while studying or upbeat music to keep my energy up. I also really enjoy conversations-especially about social issues, economics, politics, or life in general. I believe that listening, pondering and brainstorming are powerful ways to grow.

For me, learning is not just about academics or technical skills-it's about understanding others, the world, and myself every day. That's why I created this website: not only to showcase my projects, but also to share my journey, thoughts, and maybe connect with others who care about the same things.

Thanks for stopping by. I hope you find something here that resonates with you!
